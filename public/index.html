Common questions

Things people ask
before they reach out.

Do your prices include ad spend? +
No — and we will always be clear about this. Our management fee covers the work: strategy, content creation, posting, and reporting. Your ad spend is a separate budget paid directly to the platform (Facebook, Google, Instagram). We never touch your ad budget — you own it completely.
Where is your team located? +
Skyen Systems is headquartered in Bahrain, with a regional development center that gives us a large, highly skilled team across design, development, and strategy. All client communication happens during US-friendly hours. You will always have a dedicated point of contact who speaks fluent English and responds promptly.
How do payments work? +
For one-time packages: 50% upfront to begin, 50% on final delivery before handoff. For monthly retainers: billed at the start of each month. We accept all major payment methods including bank transfer and card. No long-term contracts on retainers — cancel with 30 days notice.
What is your revision policy? +
Every package includes a defined number of revision rounds — typically two full rounds of feedback after the first draft. We scope this clearly before starting so there are no misunderstandings. If a project grows significantly in scope during development, we discuss it openly and agree on any adjustment before proceeding.
What if I need something not listed? +
Just ask. Our packages cover the most common needs, but we build custom quotes for projects that fall outside them. Book a free 30-minute discovery call and tell us what you're trying to build. There's no pitch and no pressure — just a conversation to see if we're the right fit.
How is Skyen Systems different from hiring a freelancer? +
A freelancer is one person — limited by their time, their specific skill set, and their availability. Skyen Systems is a team. We have designers, developers, content writers, and strategists who work together on your project. You get team-level output, a single point of contact, and a company that will be here when you need ongoing support — not someone who might go offline for a month.
