# SECTION 6

## SEO Strategy
Keyword architecture, on-page rules, and technical requirements.

### 6.1 Core Positioning
Skyen Systems targets US small businesses searching for affordable, high-quality digital services. We win on geo-intent + service keywords that the large agencies don't fight for - and we build authority through PSEB credibility and deep service content.

### 6.3 On-Page SEO Rules
- Title tag: [Service/Topic] for Small Businesses | Skyen Systems - under 60 characters. Keyword leads.
- Meta description: Benefit sentence + CTA - under 155 characters.
- One H1 per page containing primary keyword.
- H2s contain secondary keywords naturally.
- All images: descriptive alt text with keyword where appropriate.
- Internal links: every page links to at least 3 other pages.
- Schema: Organization (homepage), Service (service pages), Product (product pages), Article (blog posts).

### 6.4 Technical SEO Checklist
- HTTPS enforced - mandatory
- XML sitemap - submitted to Google Search Console on launch
- robots.txt - properly configured, block /api/* and admin routes
- Canonical tags on all pages - prevents duplicate content
- Open Graph tags - every page, for social sharing previews
- Core Web Vitals targets: LCP < 2.5s, CLS < 0.1, INP < 200ms
- Mobile-first - Google indexes mobile version first
- Google Analytics 4 - conversion event on contact form submit
- Google Search Console - connected and verified day one
- Google Business Profile - both offices listed and verified
